# My First Mount

Started playing classic wow with the intention of hitting level 30 and getting a mount within a month (paid for 1 month membership and wasn't sure how much I would play). Since that point I have had 3 other friends join in on playing WoW, and the experience has been much better than I imagined!

When I first saw the gold cost I was really concerned I would never hit it. Learnt skinning and leatherworking early on just to try keep my gold levels higher than normal, but still was only upto around 8-10 gold.

>Baddass warrior music plays

Then I met two people on my levelling journey toward 30. First person was Sunder, a level 70 warrior I was casually inspecting the gear of in stormwind to see what sort of equipment I could expect once I levelled up. To my surprise he was wearing the exact same Red Linen shirt I was (from the quest in Elwynn forest!). Sunder helped (and has continued to help) me out with warrior related advice. He even gifted me 20 gold to help in questing and toward getting my whirlwind axe!

> A dark elf approaches

The second person was a dark elf I met in darkshire while defeating werewolves for a quest. We were both getting bad luck on the spawn rates so decided to group up. He told me of how he had been playing as a highschooler when the game first released, and how he still preffered the Classic experience as an adult today.

His name was Gerthbrookz, and before he left to continue a different quest he gave me 100 gold. I was shocked, but he seemed more than happy to help out someone playing through for the first time. So thank you, if I hadn't gotten the gold once I hit level 30 with my friend we would not have been able to get the mounts at the same time.

> The levelling grind

It took quite a few nights of continued gameplay after that, ranking slowly from level 24, 25, 26, 27. Getting both me and my friends levels up in sync, levelling through both Lakeshire and Darkshire quests so we could unlock our first mounts together. Once we were finally nearing 30 I was excited to share I had enough gold for both of use to get the mounts at the same time.

We headed over to Elwynn forest together to the lumber mill (righ next to the red shirt quest haha), and into the area with the houses and riding trainer. My friend gave me the honor of levelling up first and unlocking the mount. We both ended up choosing the Chestnut Mare (most badass), and just ran around for quite a while once we had it unlocked, really happy to finally get that significant movement boost after walking everywhere for so long.

Next stop for us now was levelling up for our next dungeon, Gnomeregan

# My First Mount

Started playing classic wow with the intention of hitting level 30 and getting a mount within a month (paid for 1 month membership and wasn't sure how much I would play). Since that point I have had 3 other friends join in on playing WoW, and the experience has been much better than I imagined!

When I first saw the gold coast I was really concerned I would never hit it. Learnt skinning and leatherworking early on just to try keep my gold levels higher than normal, but still was only upto around 8-10 gold.

Then I met two people on my levelling journey toward 30. 1 person was Sunder, a level 70 warrior who helped (and has continued to help) me out with warrior related advice. He even gifted me 20 gold to help in questing and toward getting my whirlwind axe!

The second person was a dark elf I met in darkshire while defeating werewolves for a quest. We were both getting bad luck on the spawn rates so decided to group up.

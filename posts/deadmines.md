# The Deadmines

My first Deadmines run is me and a friend trying to build a group from scratch, getting lost in the outer tunnels, but managing to push through to the final boss.

---

My first time levelling up to do the deadmines with my friend. Got quite far in a hardcore character a couple years back, but was always afraid to do many of the dungeons.

My friend has been playing a priest and is the main party leader for our two man group. Occasionally we will part up with nearby players but for this dungeon fight we wanted to make sure we were prepared without looking up any guides or video walkthroughs. Maybe even a map would have been beneficial, but I get ahead of myself.

> We are standing just outside the building near the meeting stone, to what we think is the entrance to the dungeon

He asks me to start calling others in to join our group and help us out. From what I remember loosly we need about 4 people total (did not know the cap was 5 at the time as first dungeon). So because me and him are priest and warrior, I asked in westfall chat for any damage dealers.

There were already a couple groups that were also waiting on players. We had our first damage dealer show up and join us just near the entrance, a gnome mage. Did a dance with him while we waited for another damage dealer.

All of a sudden I started getting requests from the other groups asking me and my friend the priest to join up with them and kick the gnome. I said no, quite liked the new gnome friend we had found and kept asking for a damage dealer so we could start. They got more and more insistent, two groups outbidding each other with how much gold they would give us. At one point it got up to 10 gold each just to do deadmines!

I said no thanks still though, and assured our gnome friend we would not abandon him. A level 30 rogue came by at this point, just finishing off the escort mission before deadmines. Quickly I set up a whisper to them asking if they would like to join us and they said yes! We had our group!

> Into the mines!

We all got together to run the mines. I was put in the lead as the tank and slowly we made our way though. Cleared out all the tunnels on the way to the entrance (technically I already thought this was part of the dungeon, did not realise that was behind a portal yet!). I then got lost, and ended up looping us around the same area for about 30 mins.

I felt terrible, and did not know which way to go, but everyone just kept following me. Luckily I was in a discord call with my original priest friend, and he was able to help navigate me in the right direction to then finally enter the dungeon instance (ty btw I was very concerned I would be lost forever).

Being in the mines themselves actually went quite smoothly. The hardest part for us was hitting the foundry where goblins and engineers would swarm us until we managed to have the gnome mage pull 1 goblin at a time back up the ramp where we could dunk on it and it's robot spawn.

The dark elf rogue really clutched us through most of deadmines, was a lot more experienced than us other three and had tips on how to navigate the various areas. The last sore point was with the final boss. Did not expect him to summon so many minions, and did not realise we had to target him over the adds.

Really enjoyed going through this dungeon! It was definitely a grind at some points, but pretty exciting to go through and got good loot too (smites hammer for me).
